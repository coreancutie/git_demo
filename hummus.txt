Ingredients:
1 can Garbanzo beans 
1 tablespoon lemon juice (or one lemon)
1/3 cup tahini

Steps:
Strain garbanzo beans and keep the bean juice
Peel each garbanzo bean and throw away skin and keep the bean

Blend together tahini and lemon juice in food processer
If thick, add some of the bean juice until smoother
Slowly add the peeled garbanzo beans
After all beans added, if too thick add more bean juice
If desired, add spice by incorperating cayenne pepper and blend

Pour into container and top off with cayenne pepper and olive oil

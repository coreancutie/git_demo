Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup yogurt (unflavored)
1/2 cup filtered water
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast

Steps:
Warm the water by putting it for ~30 seconds in microwave 
Add yeast to water and let sit for 15 minutes (until yeast starts to bubble)
Mix flour and yogurt together
Add the water to the dough slowly and mix until fully incorperated
Cover dough and let sit for 1 hour

Cut dough into 8-12 pieces
Roll out each piece
Cook each one individually on a cast iron pan with oil
Coat each naan with melted butter
